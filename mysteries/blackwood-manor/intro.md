# Murder at Blackwood Manor

The year is 1923. You are a detective called to Blackwood Manor on a cold autumn evening.

Lord Richard Blackwood, wealthy industrialist and patriarch of the Blackwood family, has been found dead in his study. The cause: poison.

It happened during a dinner party. Six guests were present, and any one of them could be the killer.

---

**The Victim**

Lord Richard Blackwood, 58 years old. Found slumped over his desk at 8:30 PM by the butler. A half-empty glass of brandy sat beside him.

**The Suspects**

- His wife, Lady Margaret
- His business partner, James Hartley
- The family physician, Dr. Elizabeth Chen
- The butler, Thomas Reed
- A young artist, Victoria Sterling

**Your Task**

Explore the manor. Examine each room for clues. Interrogate the suspects. Everyone has secrets.

When you're ready, make your accusation. You must identify:
- **WHO** committed the murder
- **HOW** they did it (the weapon)
- **WHY** they did it (the motive)

---

*Trust no one. Everyone has something to hide.*

*Good luck, Detective.*
