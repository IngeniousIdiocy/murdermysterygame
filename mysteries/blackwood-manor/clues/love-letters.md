# Clue: Love Letters

## Name
Love Letters

## Type
evidence

## Brief Description
A bundle of romantic letters between Lord Blackwood and Victoria Sterling.

## Detailed Description
A bundle of letters tied with a blue ribbon, hidden in a book in the library. The letters are from Victoria Sterling to Lord Blackwood, written over the past year. They speak of passionate love, secret meetings, and plans for the future. The most recent letter, dated last week, mentions that "our child will bind us together forever" and begs Richard to "tell Margaret soon."

## Significance
These prove the affair between Lord Blackwood and Victoria Sterling. They also reveal Victoria's pregnancy and suggest that Lady Margaret had motive to kill her husband before he could divorce her and leave her with nothing.
