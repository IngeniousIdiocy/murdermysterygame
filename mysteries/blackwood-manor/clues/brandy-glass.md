# Clue: Brandy Glass with Residue

## Name
Brandy Glass with Residue

## Type
evidence

## Brief Description
A crystal brandy snifter with a small amount of amber liquid remaining.

## Detailed Description
An ornate crystal brandy glass found on Lord Blackwood's desk, next to where his body was discovered. A small amount of brandy remains at the bottom, along with a faint white residue that wasn't in the original brandy. The glass has been handled - fingerprints are smudged.

## Significance
This was the murder weapon's delivery method. The poison was added to Lord Blackwood's evening brandy. Analysis would reveal digitalis residue.
